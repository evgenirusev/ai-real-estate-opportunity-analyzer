# PROMPT INPUT 2 — Location & Macro-Economic Intelligence

> **Purpose:** Deep-research prompt that gathers city/region-level economic, demographic, and infrastructure data for the location where the property exists. This contextualizes the property within its broader environment.

---

## Instructions

Run this prompt for each unique city/town in your property shortlist. The output is reusable for all properties in the same location.

---

```
PROMPT INPUT 2 — LOCATION & MACRO-ECONOMIC DEEP RESEARCH
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

You are a real estate market research analyst specializing in
Bulgarian property markets. Given the following location, conduct
a thorough deep research and return ALL data points below with
sources where possible.

TARGET LOCATION: [City / Town / Municipality name]
REGION: [Oblast / Province]
DATE OF RESEARCH: [YYYY-MM-DD]

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION A — DEMOGRAPHIC PROFILE
................................
A1. Current population (city / municipality)
A2. Population trend (last 5 and 10 years — growing / stable / declining)
A3. Population growth rate (% per year)
A4. Net migration rate (internal + international)
A5. Age distribution breakdown (0-18, 18-35, 35-55, 55+)
A6. Median age
A7. Number of households
A8. Average household size
A9. Urbanization rate in the municipality
A10. Student population (if university city)
A11. Expat / foreign resident population size and trend

SECTION B — ECONOMIC INDICATORS
................................
B1. Average monthly salary (gross and net, EUR)
B2. Median monthly salary (if available)
B3. Salary growth trend (last 3-5 years)
B4. Unemployment rate (current and 3-year trend)
B5. Major employers / industries in the area
B6. Top 5 largest companies by employee count
B7. Economic diversification index (single-industry vs diversified)
B8. GDP per capita (regional)
B9. Regional GDP growth rate
B10. Foreign direct investment (FDI) presence and trend
B11. IT / outsourcing sector presence (yes / no, scale)
B12. Tourism contribution to local economy (% and trend)
B13. Seasonal economy dependency (low / moderate / high)
B14. New business registrations trend
B15. Poverty rate in the municipality

SECTION C — INFRASTRUCTURE & DEVELOPMENT
.........................................
C1. Major infrastructure projects planned or under construction
    (highways, metro, rail, airport expansion, etc.)
C2. EU-funded development projects in the area
C3. Public transport quality rating (excellent / good / fair / poor)
C4. Internet / broadband penetration and average speed
C5. Proximity to major highway (km)
C6. Proximity to international airport (km)
C7. Rail connectivity (domestic and international)
C8. Hospital and healthcare facilities quality
C9. University presence (yes / no, ranking, student count)
C10. International schools (yes / no, count)
C11. Planned commercial developments (malls, business parks)
C12. Smart city / digitalization initiatives
C13. Renewable energy projects in the region

SECTION D — QUALITY OF LIFE & LIVABILITY
.........................................
D1. Cost of living index (relative to Sofia = 100)
D2. Safety / crime index
D3. Air quality index (AQI average)
D4. Green spaces per capita
D5. Cultural amenities (museums, theaters, events)
D6. Nightlife and dining scene rating
D7. Walkability score
D8. Climate summary (avg temps, sunshine hours, rainfall)
D9. Healthcare access rating
D10. Overall livability ranking (if available)

SECTION E — TOURISM & SHORT-TERM RENTAL POTENTIAL
..................................................
E1. Annual tourist arrivals (domestic and international)
E2. Tourism growth trend (last 3-5 years)
E3. Peak tourist seasons
E4. Average hotel/Airbnb occupancy rate
E5. Average daily rate (ADR) for short-term rentals
E6. Airbnb / Booking.com listing count in area
E7. Major tourist attractions nearby
E8. UNESCO / heritage sites within 50 km
E9. Beach / ski resort / spa proximity
E10. Event / conference tourism (yes / no, scale)

SECTION F — POLITICAL & GOVERNANCE STABILITY
.............................................
F1. Local government effectiveness rating
F2. Municipal budget health (surplus / deficit trend)
F3. Political stability of municipality
F4. Corruption perception in local government
F5. Speed of bureaucratic processes (building permits, etc.)
F6. Planned regulatory changes affecting real estate

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

OUTPUT FORMAT: Return each data point with its value and a
confidence level (confirmed / estimated / unavailable).
Include source URLs where possible.
```
