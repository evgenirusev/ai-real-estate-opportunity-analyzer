# PROMPT INPUT 3 — Real Estate Market Dynamics

> **Purpose:** Captures the supply-demand dynamics, pricing trends, and competitive landscape of the specific real estate market surrounding the property.

---

## Instructions

Run this prompt for each unique location/property type combination. Can be combined with Input 2 if researching the same location.

---

```
PROMPT INPUT 3 — REAL ESTATE MARKET DYNAMICS DEEP RESEARCH
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

You are a real estate market analyst. Research and return the
following market-level data for the specified location.

TARGET LOCATION: [City / District / Neighborhood]
PROPERTY TYPE FOCUS: [Apartment / House / Commercial / Land]
DATE OF RESEARCH: [YYYY-MM-DD]

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

SECTION A — PRICING TRENDS
...........................
A1. Average price per sq m (current, EUR) — by property type
A2. Price per sq m trend (1-year, 3-year, 5-year, 10-year)
A3. Annual price appreciation rate (%)
A4. Price-to-income ratio (local average)
A5. Price-to-rent ratio (local average)
A6. Price comparison vs Sofia (index)
A7. Price comparison vs national average (index)
A8. Price segment breakdown:
    - Budget (under €500/sq m)
    - Mid-range (€500-€1,200/sq m)
    - Premium (€1,200-€2,000/sq m)
    - Luxury (above €2,000/sq m)
A9. New-build vs resale price premium (%)
A10. Foreclosure / distressed sale volume and pricing

SECTION B — RENTAL MARKET
..........................
B1. Average monthly rent by property type and size (EUR)
B2. Rental yield (gross %) — by property type
B3. Rental yield (net %) — by property type
B4. Rent growth trend (1-year, 3-year, 5-year)
B5. Occupancy rate / vacancy rate (%)
B6. Average time to find a tenant (days)
B7. Tenant demand profile (students / families / professionals / expats / tourists)
B8. Short-term rental yield vs long-term rental yield
B9. Seasonal rental variation (% swing)
B10. Rent-to-income ratio for typical tenants

SECTION C — SUPPLY & DEMAND
............................
C1. Number of active listings (for sale) in area
C2. Number of active listings (for rent) in area
C3. Months of inventory (supply)
C4. Average days on market (sale)
C5. New construction permits issued (last 12 months)
C6. New construction pipeline (units under construction)
C7. Absorption rate (units sold per month)
C8. Buyer profile (local / domestic investor / foreign investor / diaspora)
C9. Seller profile (individual / developer / institutional)
C10. Market phase assessment (recovery / expansion / hyper-supply / recession)

SECTION D — COMPARABLE TRANSACTIONS
....................................
D1. Last 5 comparable sales within 1 km (price, sq m, date)
D2. Last 5 comparable rentals within 1 km (rent, sq m, date)
D3. Price dispersion (range and standard deviation in area)
D4. Highest and lowest sale price per sq m in last 12 months
D5. Transaction volume trend (number of sales, last 3 years)

SECTION E — EMERGING TRENDS & RISKS
.....................................
E1. Gentrification indicators (yes / no, stage)
E2. Upcoming zoning changes
E3. New large-scale developments that could affect supply
E4. Rent control or regulation proposals
E5. Short-term rental regulation status and trends
E6. Foreign buyer restrictions or changes
E7. Interest rate environment and mortgage availability
E8. Currency risk factors (BGN peg to EUR status)
E9. EU accession to Eurozone timeline and impact
E10. Climate-related risks to property values

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

OUTPUT FORMAT: Return each data point with value, trend
direction (↑ ↓ →), and confidence level. Include source URLs.
```
