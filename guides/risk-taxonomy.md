# Risk Taxonomy (Detailed)

> **Purpose:** Comprehensive catalog of risks specific to Bulgarian RE investment, mapped to data sources for early detection.

---

## Category 1: Market Risk

| ID | Risk | Detection Method |
|----|------|------------------|
| R1.1 | **Price bubble / overvaluation** | Price-to-rent, price-to-income ratios exceeding historical averages |
| R1.2 | **Local oversupply** | New construction pipeline vs absorption rate |
| R1.3 | **Demand shock** | Major employer closure, university downsizing |
| R1.4 | **Seasonal dependency** | Coastal/ski properties with 3-4 month seasons |
| R1.5 | **Interest rate increases** | Affects leveraged returns & buyer pool |

---

## Category 2: Property-Specific Risk

| ID | Risk | Detection Method |
|----|------|------------------|
| R2.1 | **Hidden structural defects** | Panel buildings (Soviet-era), earthquake damage, subsidence |
| R2.2 | **Renovation cost overrun** | Typical in Bulgaria: +20-40% |
| R2.3 | **Asbestos / hazardous materials** | Common in pre-1990 buildings |
| R2.4 | **Building management dysfunction** | Common in Bulgarian condos |
| R2.5 | **Utility infrastructure failure** | Aging systems in older buildings |

---

## Category 3: Income Risk

| ID | Risk | Detection Method |
|----|------|------------------|
| R3.1 | **Vacancy** | Prolonged void periods, especially off-season |
| R3.2 | **Tenant default** | Weak eviction enforcement in practice |
| R3.3 | **Rent decline** | Oversupply, economic downturn |
| R3.4 | **Bad tenant damage** | Limited landlord protection in practice |
| R3.5 | **Platform risk** | Airbnb/Booking policy changes, algorithm shifts |

---

## Category 4: Legal & Regulatory Risk

| ID | Risk | Detection Method |
|----|------|------------------|
| R4.1 | **Title defects** | Incomplete cadastral records, especially rural |
| R4.2 | **Restitution claims** | Historical property claims (declining but exists) |
| R4.3 | **Planning / zoning changes** | Can affect use or value |
| R4.4 | **Building permit issues** | Unauthorized modifications common |
| R4.5 | **Short-term rental regulation** | Municipalities may restrict |
| R4.6 | **Tax law changes** | Rental income tax, CGT, property tax changes |
| R4.7 | **EU regulatory harmonization** | May introduce new requirements |

---

## Category 5: Macro / Country Risk

| ID | Risk | Detection Method |
|----|------|------------------|
| R5.1 | **Political instability** | Government changes, policy uncertainty |
| R5.2 | **Eurozone accession delays** | Affects currency perception |
| R5.3 | **BGN peg risk** | Extremely low probability but catastrophic impact |
| R5.4 | **Corruption in local administration** | Permit delays, arbitrary fees |
| R5.5 | **Judicial system inefficiency** | Slow dispute resolution |
| R5.6 | **EU funding withdrawal** | Affects regional development |

---

## Category 6: Environmental / Force Majeure

| ID | Risk | Detection Method |
|----|------|------------------|
| R6.1 | **Earthquake** | Bulgaria is in a seismic zone (especially south) |
| R6.2 | **Flooding** | Specific areas, check municipal flood maps |
| R6.3 | **Landslides** | Hilly terrain, especially Black Sea coast |
| R6.4 | **Climate change** | Coastal erosion, drought, extreme weather |
| R6.5 | **Pollution** | Industrial legacy in some areas |

---

## Category 7: Operational Risk

| ID | Risk | Detection Method |
|----|------|------------------|
| R7.1 | **Remote management challenges** | If not locally present |
| R7.2 | **Property management quality** | Limited professional options outside Sofia |
| R7.3 | **Language barrier** | Bulgarian bureaucracy largely in Bulgarian |
| R7.4 | **Banking complexity** | Opening accounts, receiving funds |
| R7.5 | **Insurance gaps** | Underinsurance is common |

---

## Mitigation Strategies by Category

| Category | Mitigation Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| **Market** | Diversification, conservative underwriting, buffer |
| **Property** | Professional survey, structural engineer report |
| **Income** | Conservative vacancy assumptions, tenant screening |
| **Legal** | Bulgarian lawyer, title insurance (if available) |
| **Macro** | Position sizing, geographic diversification |
| **Environmental** | Due diligence, insurance, flood map check |
| **Operational** | Local partner/manager, bilingual lawyer/accountant |
